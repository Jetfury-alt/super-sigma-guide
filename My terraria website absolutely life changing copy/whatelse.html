New Difficulties

Now you've finished Normal mode, start experimenting with harder mode such as Expert or Master mode.

Supa fire tip from Lionel: There is also a significantly harder difficulty called Legendary, and is found by using the secret seed "fortheworthy" on Master mode.

There are also other secret seeds, such as:

  • "celebrationmk10": Take a break from all the stressing gameplay prior! This generates a world that offers unique and highly beneficial features for players, such as every item dropping with the best modifier:
  • "05162020" or "5162020": This is a drunk seed, making both of each evil biome present in the world as well as every ore:
  • "notthebees": Fills the entire world with bee-themed biomes, items and structures:
  • "notraps": Ironically, this seeds significantly increases traps in the generated world:
  • "dontdigup": Flips the player's progression thorugh biomes by starting them in the UNderworld first:
  • "getfixedboi": Incredibly hard, also impossible even. This seeds combines all the previous secret seeds into one, as well as a bump in difficulty:

Multiplayer

Start playing on a server or with up to 8 players per custom world!

If you have a PC: To start multiplayer, open up a multiplayer in Terraria, making sure to enable Steam Multiplayer:

When you've entered the world, press shift+tab to bring up the Steam Community overlay, for which you can invite your friends.

If you have a mobile device: To start multiplayer, open use the "Local" section to show worlds to host:

When you are joining a multiplayer world, open the "Online" section to access world online. You will need to be on the same network as the device hosting:

Mods

Mods allow you to use third party software in order to modify your experience further.

If you have a PC: To access mods, download the plugin tModLoader on Steam:

By using the Terraria Steam Workshop and subscribing to your chosen mods. tModLoader will load them onto your current or new worlds.

If you have an Android: There isn't an official mod loader made by ReLogic or 505 Games, but there are some third-party. Buy the TL Pro application off the Google Play Store.
